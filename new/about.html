Our Story

What happens when an entrepreneur exits a venture ? He starts a new venture ! Coducer is the third venture from the stables of our founder Jai Keerthi. After having been a part of two founding teams, Coducer, a services was born when Jai wanted to try his on developing multiple products for clients so that he is able to get his hands dirty with the latest in technology and at the same solve many new challenges.

With a vision to build a kick ass team with a product mindset and one that can solve problems for startups day in day out. Coducer was started. Two years down the road, we are a strong close knit team that enjoys writing code together, delighting customers and party even harder.
